What is Recycled For Kids?
Recycled for kids is an exciting new concept that brings parents looking to purchase gently used clothing, toys and accessories together with parents who have a surplus of these items that their children have grown out of.

Lets face it , our children grow so fast they barley have a chance to wear the clothes we by them. Why not pass these great items on and make a little money to put back into your childens wardrobe at the same time.

Why Participate?

  • Clean out your little one’s clothes and toys and help moms in our community buy much needed clothing and toys at bargain prices! 
  • Earn yourself between 70% and 80% for these unwanted items.
  • Sellers shop early!  Shop between 8:00am and 9:00am before the sale starts and Volunteers shop early between 8:00pm and 9:00pm following setup the night before.
  • Take pride in knowing your unwanted items put a smile on a childs face :)
