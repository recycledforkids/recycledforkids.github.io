How the Consignment works!

  • Email us for a Seller Number.
  • Tag your items. (We will send the tag template to you)
  • Bring your tagged at sorted items to the Sale. Time will be assigned. Place them in the appropriate areas that we have set up for clothing and toys.
  • We sell your items between 8-2pm.  Pick up time for any unsold items is between 4:00-5:00pm. 
  • We will send you a cheque within the 2 weeks after the sale ends.

Tagging Instructions!

Please inspect your items carefully!  Look at this as though you were going to purchase them.  Items that have stains, tears, missing buttons, missing pieces, etc. will be removed from the sale and returned to you.

  • If you would like ready made sales tags we can email them to you.
  • If you are creating your own all tags must have a seller’s number, price, description (brand, type) and size.  The seller number should be in the upper right hand corner and the price in the lower right hand corner. 
  • The minimum size of a tag is 1” by 2”, anything smaller than that will not be accepted.
  • Sizes are: 0-3 months, 3-6 months, 6-12 months, 12-24 months, 2T, 3T, 4T, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, and 12.
  • When pricing only use increments of 50 cents and dollar increments.
  • Only attach items with safety pins.  No staples or labels!  Do not use tape!   
  • For larger toys and items use cable ties.  Safety pins and wire ties can be purchased at the dollar store.
  • Please wash and clean all items.  Fold all clothing neatly.  Ensure equipment is in clean condition with all pieces attached/included.  Toys that require batteries must have batteries. (packs of batteries can be purchased at the dollar store)
  • All shoes/boots should be in brand new or in like new condition.  All shoes are to be in a clear ziplock back with the tag tapped to the outside of the bag for easy removal.
  • Items that are sets tend to sell better.  Please make sure to clearly make on your tag 2 or 3 piece set.
  • When packing boxes please fold clothes by size separated with a piece of paper or paper towel for easy sorting for you during drop off.  If you need us to we can unpack the boxes for you
  • Please mark all boxes with your seller number.  Reusable containers are allowed and will be returned to you.

Note: If you were in a sale prior and would like to use the same seller number please contact us.

This should only take a few moments of your time.  We all want our children to be safe!

Lost and Found Items

There will be a lost and found rack at the end of the sale.  Please check this area when picking up your unsold items after the sale.  Items that have no tag will not be sold.  Items that are not claimed will be donated.
Pricing your items

  • Please try to be realistic when pricing your items.  Think of what you would pay if you were purchasing these items.
  • Try to price your items 25 to 40% off the original sale price.
